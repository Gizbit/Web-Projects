Paragon Cosmetics was founded in 1978. Over the years the company became a household name for true Australian Manufacturer of quality skin care and hair care range. Because of our ethical reputation, Paragon's custom services are sought by major international cosmetic houses.

Paragon's product range includes Fragrances, Skin Care, Hair Care and Toileteries and our products are 100 per cent Australian made and owned. We also operate as a contract manufacturer for private labels.

Our philosophy is to create exquisite range of beautifying, anti- ageing specialities for those who treasure their appearance. At Paragon, We always strive to develop affordable quality products for our local and International customers.
